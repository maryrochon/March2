Assignment 1: Poster

The objective of this assignment was to make a 24" by 36" poster about either our life under covid-19 or how we see the future of design. However there was a catch. We couldn't use any design applications, like Adobe.

I chose to go the direction of life under/with covid, and how so much has been happening in the world that it can be overwhelming. But at the same time I wanting to depict the feeling of slowly becoming numb to big news as well because of this reason. Therefore my goal was to create a choatic poster with multiple news articles headlines. This process was very time consuming and made me remember a lot of events from the last two years that I forgot. I experimented with both pages and my desktop. The pages version is my favourite, as it has the most common words watermarked overtop of the news articles, adding to the chaos. I attempting to do the same thing with the desktop version but it appeared out of place.
